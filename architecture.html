Protocol Queries

Common clarifications regarding the system.

Are the generated memories permanent?
By default, all sequences are ephemeral. They exist only within the active session buffer. To retain a sequence, you must explicitly 'anchor' it to your private vault (available on Dreamer tier), otherwise it is deleted upon disconnection.
How is the neural model trained?
FugueState is trained on a proprietary dataset of public domain literature, abstract visual art, and synthesized emotional telemetry. We do not use user data for training unless you opt-in to the "Collective Unconscious" program.
Can I export to other formats?
Yes. Visual data can be exported as PNG, HEIC, or MP4. Narrative data is available as JSON, Markdown, or raw text. API users have access to the raw tensor data for custom interpretation.
Is this safe for biological memory?
While we strive for emotional fidelity, FugueState constitutes a simulation. Prolonged exposure may result in "reality drift." We recommend limiting sessions to 45 minutes and taking regular breaks to ground yourself in physical reality.
