01

Connect Your Sources

Link your digital archives—Gmail, Google Drive, Notion, local files, or custom MCP integrations. Your data remains private and under your control. We analyse patterns without ever storing your content externally.

02

Enter the State

Begin your journey by selecting your Muse—an AI personality that will interpret your memories. Choose between the Analyst, Poet, Visualist, or Narrator, each offering a unique lens through which to see your data.

03

Let Dameris Dream

As you chat with Dameris, she analyses your memories using multiple AI models selected at random, ensuring each response is unique and non-generic. She identifies emotional patterns, thematic connections, and hidden narratives within your digital life. The AI doesn't just search—it interprets.

04

Reflect on Patterns

Browse your curated Archive of memory fragments. See recurring themes, forgotten connections, and emotional arcs visualised as creative expressions. Each piece tells a story you might have missed.

05

Create New Dreams

Use the Visualise mode to generate new imagery from your memories. Remix existing artefacts. Export your creations. Transform data exhaust into living expressions that evolve with you.
