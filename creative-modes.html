5 New Creative Modes

Collage Mode

Creates visual and textual collages by layering memory fragments into cohesive mixed-media compositions. Choose from textual, visual, or hybrid styles to juxtapose fragments in unexpected ways.

Style Options
Textual, Visual, Hybrid
Output
Collage with moodboard
Use Case
Mixed-media art

Dream Mode

Surreal & Symbolic

Transforms memories into surreal, dreamlike narratives with symbolic interpretations. Adjust intensity from subtle whispers to extreme distortions to explore the subconscious patterns in your fragments.

Intensity Levels
Subtle → Extreme
Output
Dream narrative + symbols
Use Case

Remix Mode

Recombine & Transform

Recombines fragments using different creative techniques: juxtapose contrasts, blend meanings, transform contexts, or collide ideas. Generates variations to explore multiple creative directions.

Techniques
Juxtapose, Blend, Transform, Collide
Output
Remixed creation + variations
Formats
Narrative, Poem, Dialogue, etc.
Use Case
Experimental writing

Echo Mode

Patterns & Obsessions

Analyzes recurring patterns, persistent themes, and recurring questions across your entire memory collection. Reveals what echoes through time—your creative obsessions and evolving inquiries.

Time Ranges
Week, Month, Year, All
Output
Patterns + themes + questions
Use Case
Self-analysis
